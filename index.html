The Engagement

Jason Leung

Born in Hong Kong and raised as a Kiwi, Jason has always loved reading and puzzles. For Mia's 17th birthday, he designed a treasure hunt leading to a teddy that still sits on their sofa today. Jason went on to study Computer Science at MIT in Boston and finally found his way back to Mia afer 4.5 years. He loves playing poker and boardgames with friends, flying his drone and planning trips with Mia.

Mia Zhang

Mia was born in Xi'an China, she grew up in Auckland New Zealand and loved to sing and dance. She met Jason when she was 16 in Highschool. The first movie they watched together was Pixar's "UP" in 2009, and like the movie Mia hopes to travel the world with Jason. Mia studied medicine at Griffith in the Gold Coast and now works in Ophthalmology. She loves animals, painting, snorkelling and Jason.

Our Story

  • 2011 Brisbane

    Jason gets accepted to UQ med and joins Mia in Brisbane. They cuddle Koalas and feed Kangaroos. The green band aid on Jason’s finger is from a koala scratch.

  • 2012 Great Barrier Reef

    Taking inspiration from Finding Nemo, Jason and Mia travel to the Great Barrier reef during uni break. Later this year Jason gets accepted to MIT and they start long distance between Brisbane and Boston.

  • 2013 Hong Kong

    Mia meets Jason’s parents and relatives for Chinese New Year, they also get to travel to Macau and Shen Zhen. Macau has the best egg tarts in the world.

  • 2014 Cairns

    Back to the water, this time chasing waterfalls down river rapids and trekking in lush rainforests. Followed by a trip to the concrete jungle that is New York. We decided we prefer our jungles in green.

  • 2015 Disney World, Florida

    Mia visits Jason in Boston, and he surprised her with a thanksgiving trip to Disney world and Universal Studios in Orlando, Florida.

  • 2016 Gold Coast

    During this year both Jason and Mia graduate from university. Mia attends his PBE fraternity ball in Boston, Jason attends her Medball at Movieworld, Gold Coast. Jason and Mia both find jobs in Sydney, so ends their long distance.

  • 2017 San Francisco

    Mia joins Jason in San Francisco after coming back from volunteering in the Pacific Islands. They bike across the Golden Gate bridge while extremely jetlagged. They adopt the best dog in the world, Roxy.

  • 2018 Sydney

    Jason and Mia throw their first Christmas party together in their apartment in Sydney. They finally feel like they have a place to call home where they can spend their days together.

  • 2019 Tokyo

    Jason and Mia get fat. Almost every photo from this trip was of food. They hiked all over Tasmania later this year to balance out the overeating from Japan. They also get engaged!

  • 2020 Queenstown

    They go back to their roots in New Zealand and realise how beautiful the place is. Shortly afterwards the era of lockdowns begins.

  • 2021 Hamilton Islands

    Started out the year with a ray of hope as small travel bubbles open. Hamilton Island was a beautiful place to unwind. Jason and Mia finally start thinking about planning their wedding, and they hope to be able to share it with you!
